## Design Context

### Users
Mobile-first bakery and breakfast menu visitors who are scanning food, prices, hours, and photos quickly.

### Brand Personality
Editorial, restrained, typographic, with a raw bakery-menu character rather than generic restaurant polish.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light paper-like menu surface, narrow mobile composition, monochrome typography, fine borders, and photographic food moments.

### Design Principles
- Keep the menu typography distinctive, but never at the expense of readability.
- Use tight tracking only for large display moments; small labels and uppercase text need neutral or slightly open tracking.
- Preserve the compact editorial menu rhythm with precise spacing and minimal decoration.
- Treat photos as useful inspection content, not decorative filler.
